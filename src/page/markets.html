Our Markets

The world needs the solutions we provide, and we are committed to developing groundbreaking solutions for a wide variety of industries.

Driven by more than 100 years of innovation, we collaborate with customers around the world across a wide range of industries. For decades, our innovations have improved the quality of life for consumers. Our materials go into everything from can coatings for the food you eat to the fibers in your clothing and home textiles. Our resins make housewares dishwasher safe, tough, and stylish and high-performance medical devices more durable. We provide organic acids that replace antibiotics in animal feed. And our polymers are in low-emissions coatings for homes and cars.

We’re ushering in a new era of innovation while helping our partners solve society’s greatest sustainability challenges. Our innovative films enable electric vehicles to be lighter weight for greater range and are used for next-generation technology like head-up displays for windshields to improve safety. We’re also a world leader at developing fiber from sustainably sourced wood pulp to make sustainable clothing, biobased plastic for eyewear, and compostable food packaging that won’t harm the planet.

These are just some examples of the industries where Eastman materials are being used. Explore the Markets where we are making an impact.
